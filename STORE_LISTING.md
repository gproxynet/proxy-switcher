## Detailed description (listing body)
Proxy Switcher & Manager is a fast, no-nonsense proxy switcher for Chrome. Switch
between your proxies in one click from the toolbar — HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS4 and
SOCKS5, with username/password authentication that just works.

Built for people who actually live in proxies: web scraping, QA, ad verification,
affiliate and multi-account workflows.

★ WHAT IT DOES
• One-click proxy switching — Direct, System, or any profile you saved.
• Full protocol support — HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS4, SOCKS5, with proxy authentication.
• Bulk import — paste a whole proxy list (one per line) or load a .txt file.
  Formats: host:port · host:port:user:pass · user:pass@host:port ·
  scheme://user:pass@host:port. Duplicates are skipped automatically.
• Country flags — every proxy is auto-labeled with its country and city.
• Keyboard shortcuts — Ctrl+Shift+X toggles the proxy on/off, Ctrl+Shift+Z goes
  direct. Switch without touching the mouse.
• Per-profile bypass rules — keep localhost and internal hosts off the proxy.
• Live IP & location check — see exactly which exit you're on.
• Badge indicator — a colored ON badge shows a proxy is active at a glance.

★ PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No analytics, no tracking, no remote code. Your proxy profiles stay in local
browser storage on your machine. Credentials are sent only to the proxy you
configured, only when it asks for them.

★ WORKS WITH ANY PROVIDER
Use any proxy from any source — residential, mobile, datacenter or your own
servers. Paste your list and go.
